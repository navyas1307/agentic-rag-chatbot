RETURNS AND REFUNDS POLICY

Return Window
We offer a 30-day return window from the date of delivery for most products. Products must be unused, in original packaging, with all tags and labels intact.

Eligible Items
The following items are eligible for return:
- Clothing and fashion accessories
- Electronics (unopened and unused)
- Home and kitchen items
- Books and stationery
- Footwear (unworn with original packaging)

Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned:
- Intimate wear and innerwear
- Personalized or customized products
- Perishable goods
- Digital products and gift cards
- Opened cosmetics or personal care items

How to Initiate a Return
1. Log in to your account on our website
2. Go to "My Orders" section
3. Select the order you want to return
4. Click "Return Item" and select the reason
5. Our team will approve the return within 24 hours
6. Schedule a pickup or drop off at nearest location

Return Pickup
We offer free return pickup for orders above ₹999. For orders below this amount, a pickup fee of ₹50 will be charged.

Refund Process
Once we receive and inspect your returned item:
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
- Money is refunded to the original payment method
- Bank transfers may take an additional 3-5 business days
- Store credit refunds are instant

Exchange Policy
We offer exchanges for size or color variations within the 30-day window. The exchange process is the same as returns, and we'll ship the new item once we receive the returned product.

Damaged or Defective Products
If you receive a damaged or defective product:
- Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
- Provide photos of the damaged item
- We will arrange immediate replacement or full refund
- No return shipping charges for damaged items

Quality Issues
If the product has quality issues:
- Report within 7 days of delivery
- We may request photos or videos
- Replacement or refund will be provided after verification
- No questions asked for genuine quality concerns

Cancellation Policy
Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement at no charge. After 24 hours, standard return policy applies if the item has already shipped.
