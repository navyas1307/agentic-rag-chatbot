PAYMENT METHODS AND INFORMATION

Accepted Payment Methods

Credit and Debit Cards
We accept all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and RuPay. Payments are processed securely through our payment gateway.

UPI (Unified Payments Interface)
Pay instantly using UPI apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and BHIM. Simply scan the QR code or enter your UPI ID at checkout.

Net Banking
We support net banking from all major banks in India including:
- State Bank of India
- HDFC Bank
- ICICI Bank
- Axis Bank
- Kotak Mahindra Bank
- And 50+ other banks

Digital Wallets
Pay using popular digital wallets:
- Paytm Wallet
- PhonePe Wallet
- Amazon Pay
- Mobikwik
- Freecharge

Cash on Delivery (COD)
Cash on Delivery is available for orders up to ₹50,000. COD orders below ₹1,000 have a handling fee of ₹50. COD is not available for all pin codes - check availability at checkout.

Buy Now Pay Later
We partner with Simpl, LazyPay, and ZestMoney to offer Buy Now Pay Later options. Split your payment into 3 interest-free installments.

EMI Options
Convert purchases above ₹3,000 into easy EMIs:
- 3 months: No Cost EMI available
- 6 months: No Cost EMI available
- 9 months: Interest charges apply
- 12 months: Interest charges apply

International Payments
For international customers, we accept:
- International Credit/Debit Cards
- PayPal
- Wire Transfer (for bulk orders)

Payment Security
All payments are processed through 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your complete card details. Our payment gateway is PCI-DSS compliant ensuring maximum security.

Payment Issues

Failed Transactions
If your payment fails but amount is deducted:
- The amount will be automatically refunded within 5-7 business days
- Contact your bank if refund is delayed beyond 10 days
- Email us at payments@company.com with transaction details

Payment Confirmation
You will receive payment confirmation via:
- Email to your registered email ID
- SMS to your registered mobile number
- Order confirmation on the website

Refund Methods
Refunds are processed to the original payment method:
- Card payments: 5-7 business days
- UPI/Wallet: 2-3 business days
- Net Banking: 5-7 business days
- COD orders: Bank transfer or store credit within 7 days

Pricing and Currency
All prices are listed in Indian Rupees (₹). International customers will see prices in their local currency at checkout with applicable conversion rates.

Taxes and Charges
- GST is included in the displayed price
- Delivery charges are added at checkout
- No hidden charges or fees

Offers and Discounts
- Discount codes can be applied at checkout
- Only one discount code per order
- Offers cannot be combined unless specified
- Bank-specific offers available on select cards
