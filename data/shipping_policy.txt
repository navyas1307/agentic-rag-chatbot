SHIPPING POLICY

Standard Shipping
We offer standard shipping for all orders within India. Standard shipping typically takes 4-7 business days for delivery. Orders are processed within 1-2 business days before shipment.

Express Shipping
Express shipping is available for urgent orders and delivers within 2-3 business days. Express shipping charges are ₹150 for orders under ₹2,000 and free for orders above ₹2,000.

Free Shipping
We provide free standard shipping on all orders above ₹999. Orders below this amount will have a shipping fee of ₹75.

International Shipping
We currently ship to select international destinations including USA, UK, UAE, Canada, and Australia. International shipping takes 7-14 business days and costs vary based on location and weight.

Shipping Zones
- Zone 1 (Metro cities): 2-3 business days
- Zone 2 (Tier 2 cities): 3-5 business days  
- Zone 3 (Rural areas): 5-7 business days

Order Tracking
Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order on our website or the courier partner's website.

Shipping Partners
We work with reliable courier partners including BlueDart, Delhivery, and India Post to ensure safe and timely delivery.

Shipping Restrictions
Some products may have shipping restrictions based on size, weight, or destination. Hazardous materials and certain electronic items cannot be shipped to all locations.

Delivery Attempts
Our courier partners will make up to 3 delivery attempts. If delivery fails after 3 attempts, the order will be returned to us and a refund will be processed.
